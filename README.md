### Emma's Game

Name's and plays an animal based on keyboard strokes.

Great with one of these connected to a TV or a projector:

### How to play

1. Visit https://alshakero.github.io/emmas-game/ on your device of choice.
2. Press number keys on the keyboard or buttons on the screen to name and play an animal's video.
